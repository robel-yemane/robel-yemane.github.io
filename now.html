It's been a long while since I updated this page. A lot has happened since my last update. The biggest and best of all being I now have a 10-month-old baby girl. As anyone who has a kid can attest, your life changes unrecognisably... to the good. It's a marvelous thing to happen to anyone and I am savouring it. Of course, it comes with its own challenges, but that's life!

My new routine:

-Wake up very early
