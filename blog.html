I am a Software Engineer (DevOps, if that's a thing) living and working in London. I moved to the UK in 2010 from Eritrea where I was born and lived for ~ 2/3 of my life.

In 2014 I graduated from the University of Westminster in Computer Networks and Comms (more on that on my LinkedIn).

Interested in Computers, troubleshooting from a young age, I have formalised that interest by getting a degree in Computer Networks. Multilingual - English, Tigrigna, Italian and French. The last two I'm quite forgetting, shamefully. The plan is to practice them, but in reality I'm losing them by the day.

Lately I have been really interested in the philosophy of Stoicism. I find that it answers many of the question that I come up about my life, emotions and the world in general. I may write about this in the form of blogs or musings etc... In the meantime I am reading one letter each morning from Seneca's letters to Lucillium (Epistulae Morales ad Lucilium)[link to TF's ].

What's the plan with this website? Whilst in the lockdown I thought I could teach myself web dev, something I've always postponed or was never as interested. I've mostly if not entirely done backend development. Voila' I decided to get a website and start developing. The plan is to keep improving this site as I go and learn more about HTML, CSS, JS etc. It is also my plan to do everything by hand, 'the hard way' etc. and keep this site as minimalist as possible [like this https://mnmlist.com/about/ :D ].
